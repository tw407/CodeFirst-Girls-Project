This website is here to help you survive Maths at Bath. We are three final year students who have been through the highs and lows of studying a Maths degree at this wonderful university.
The aim of this website is to give you an insight into what it's like to study here and to offer any advice if you are struggling. This can be found on our FAQs page.
However, if you feel that it is just getting too much and you need to distance yourself from Maths then please visit the Destress page for a fun surprise...
