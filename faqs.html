What do I do if I miss a lecture?

Don't worry too much - make sure you get the notes off your more organised mate ASAP and copy them up. Get them to send a pic of their notes over FB or WhatsApp to save time. If you keep asking the same person for notes all the time they may get annoyed with you - buy them cheap chocolate from Fresh and they won't mind anymore hehe :)

I can't do any problem sheets. Where can I get help?

Your tutors in your tutorials! They're mostly normal and approachable people (one of them has designed this website and is writing this right now hehe...) Also we think it helps to study with friends. Teaching each other can aid your learning. If this does not help, there is a handy drop-in session - MASH. Here, you can get help from maths lecturers and older students, who have been through it all before.

I'm worried Maths is not for me...

No fear. Talk to your personal tutor - it's what they're there for! If you really don't like it, there may be an option to change courses. But we believe you can make it through, see how you do in the Jan exams - you might surprise yourself!

OMG I've ran out of money - what do I do?

Call your parents. Or get a part-time job in the SU/ in Fresh/ in town. Whatever you do, don't get a WONGA loan.

I'm in final year and keep bursting into tears randomly - what's wrong with me?

First, well done for getting to final year. Well we're not doctors but it's probably because you're stressed as fu really stressed. It happens to us three all the time. It's normal to be stressed - if you aren't you're probably doing something wrong/ are gonna fail/ are a genius. We have no advice for geniuses - sorry? Sometimes it's good to get the emotions out - maybe next time find a more healthy outlet e.g. the gym or some other physical activity...
